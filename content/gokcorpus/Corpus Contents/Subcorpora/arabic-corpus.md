The Genealogies of Knowledge Arabic corpus features a wide range of translations of and commentaries on ancient Greek texts, as well as original works by Alfarabi, Averroes and others that are inspired by Aristotelian and Neoplatonist thought. The current size of the Arabic subcorpus is 3,082,786 words.
